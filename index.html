Where would you go?

If I could take a vacation, I would take it in Summer and I would travel to Amsterdam, Netherlands, a city famous for its historic canals, beautiful architecture, and vibrant culture. I would explore the Anne Frank House, the Van Gogh Museum with its lively painting, and the Rijksmuseum to experience art and history. I would walk and bike along the canal belt to allow me to see the city from a native’s perspective. Amsterdam is filled with charming streets, colorful houses, and scenic waterways that make it a perfect destination for creating memorable vacation (Iamsterdam, n.d.).

Figure 1. Van Gogh with photo credits inside museum with vitrines. Reprinted from Musement.com (2025)

What would you do?

During my vacation, I would enjoy several exciting activities:

  • Take a canal cruise to explore Amsterdam from the water
  • Ride a bike along the city’s picturesque streets
  • Visit local markets such as Albert Cuyp Market for shopping and souvenirs
  • Explore nearby parks and historical neighborhoods on foot

Figure 2. Canal cruise in Amsterdam. Reprinted from Canalsofamsterdam.com (2025)

What would you eat?

Sampling local cuisine would be a highlight of my trip. As a sweet tooth, I would find it at home. I would try poffertjes, small fluffy pancakes typically served with powdered sugar and butter, and stroopwafels, sweet caramel-filled waffle cookies. I would also enjoy fresh Dutch cheeses, herring, and other street foods available throughout the city. Pairing these treats with a cup of Dutch coffee or a refreshing soft drink would complete the culinary experience (DutchAmsterdam, 2023). Eating local food while exploring the city would make the trip even more unforgettable.
